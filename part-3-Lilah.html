Jessica the Pet Rock

Over the years, Jessica had two kids, Lilah and Eren. One day, I decided that I didn't like Eren's texture that much, so I told my brother to put Eren outside. A few hours late, I realized I had given him Lilah instead! I asked him where he put my rock and he said he chucked it over the fence. So I made my dad, brother, and me go search for Lilah in the neighbor's yard. On the third day of looking, I saw a big spider and called off the search. Lilah was never found.
